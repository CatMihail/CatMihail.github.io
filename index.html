01 / 2025 · AI (II)

Building Video Super-Resolution from the Ground Up: A Modular Pipeline Approach

Alignment-based VSR decisively beats single-image methods — our enhanced FRVSR (16 residual blocks) hit PSNR 33.24/SSIM 0.924, and adding PCD deformable convolutions pushed that further to 33.68/SSIM 0.929. Fine-tuning on real H.265 compressed content proved essential — without it, models collapsed under heavy compression (PSNR crashing from 28.90 at QP17 to 24.17 at QP37). Integrating a spatio-temporal discriminator with optical flow estimation delivered measurably stronger temporal consistency, though the key unsolved tension remains: every accuracy gain costs real-time speed.

01 / 2026 · PICSC

Network Traffic Discrimination: VoIP vs. Non-VoIP Classification

Short flows + strict bidirectional filtering unlock peak VoIP detection — early traffic tells the whole story. Performance drops fast with longer flows, as signal fades into noise. Best results demand model-specific tuning: synthetic sampling and PCA boost neural nets, but hurt models like SVM and RF.
